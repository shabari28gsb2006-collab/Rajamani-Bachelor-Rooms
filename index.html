About Rajamani Bachelor Rooms

Rajamani Bachelor Rooms at Rajamani Complex, Srivilliputhur, offers affordable, comfortable, and secure accommodation for bachelors, working professionals, and students. Located on Madurai Road near Kamarajar Statue, the property provides excellent connectivity and easy access to important facilities in the town.

Our rooms are designed for individuals seeking a peaceful and convenient place to stay. The premises are monitored with CCTV security, ensuring a safe environment for residents. Clean common bathroom and toilet facilities are available and maintained for the comfort of tenants.
